Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts the financial teachings of the author's biological father (the "poor dad") with those of his best friend's father (the "rich dad"). Through anecdotal storytelling, Kiyosaki imparts fundamental lessons about wealth accumulation, financial independence, and the mindset needed to achieve prosperity. The book emphasizes the importance of financial education, asset ownership, and embracing risk as pathways to financial success.
